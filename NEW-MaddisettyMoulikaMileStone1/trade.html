Proof By BTS

Details

Released June 10 2022
Label BigHit

The album sold more than two million copies globally on the first day of release alone. In South Korea,
Australia, Austria, Belgium, Canada, Finland, Germany, Japan, the Netherlands, New Zealand, Poland,
Portugal, and Switzerland, it debuted at the top of the charts. Proof also made it into the top ten in
Norway, Italy, and Lithuania. It was the group's fifth top-10 album in the UK when it peaked at number
eight. They achieved their first number one in Greece with this album, which debuted at number three
and peaked at number one.
